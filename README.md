# 📊 Business Insights 360

An end-to-end Business Intelligence solution developed using **Power BI** to transform raw business data into interactive dashboards that provide actionable insights across **Finance, Sales, Marketing, Supply Chain, and Executive** functions.

---

# 📌 About the Project

Business Insights 360 is an end-to-end Business Intelligence solution developed for **AtliQ Hardware** using Power BI. The project transforms raw business data into interactive dashboards that enable stakeholders and business users to monitor business performance across Finance, Sales, Marketing, Supply Chain, and Executive functions.

The solution combines **data transformation, data modeling, DAX calculations, and interactive dashboard development** to deliver a centralized reporting platform that supports data-driven decision-making.

---

# ❗ Business Problem

As AtliQ Hardware expanded its operations, business decisions were increasingly based on fragmented reports and intuition rather than centralized data. The lack of a unified reporting solution reduced visibility into business performance and made it difficult for stakeholders to monitor key performance indicators across different business functions.

To overcome these challenges, the organization required a centralized Business Intelligence solution capable of integrating data from multiple sources into a single reporting platform.

---

# 🎯 Project Objective

The objective of this project was to transform fragmented business data into a centralized reporting solution by developing a comprehensive Power BI dashboard that delivers actionable insights, improves operational visibility, and supports strategic business decisions for stakeholders and business users.

---

# ⚙️ Methodology

1. Collected business data from a SQL Database and supporting Excel data sources.
2. Performed data cleaning and transformation using Power Query.
3. Built an optimized data model for analytical reporting.
4. Developed DAX measures and KPIs for business analysis.
5. Designed interactive dashboards for multiple business functions.
6. Implemented bookmarks, drill-through, tooltips, and dynamic filtering.
7. Published the solution to Power BI Service for interactive reporting.

---

# 🚀 Solution Developed

To address these challenges, I developed an end-to-end Power BI solution by transforming and analyzing more than **1.8 million business records** from multiple data sources.

The solution integrates Finance, Sales, Marketing, Supply Chain, and Executive reporting into a centralized Business Intelligence platform, enabling stakeholders to monitor KPIs, analyze business performance, and uncover actionable insights through interactive visualizations.
